About Us

Our History

Welcome to Zanzibar Safari Tours, your premier gateway to the enchanting world of Zanzibar's breathtaking landscapes and extraordinary wildlife. Our journey began over a decade ago, rooted in a passion for adventure and a deep love for the natural wonders of this unique region. Founded in 2010 by a group of seasoned travel enthusiasts and local conservationists, Zanzibar Safari Tours was established to provide authentic, immersive safari experiences while promoting sustainable tourism practices. From humble beginnings, offering small group tours to friends and family, our company has grown into a renowned provider of bespoke safari adventures. Our team’s dedication to preserving Zanzibar's rich biodiversity and cultural heritage has been the cornerstone of our success. Today, we are proud to be a leading name in safari tourism, known for our commitment to excellence, personalized service, and eco-friendly operations.

Our Mission

At Zanzibar Safari Tours, our mission is to offer unforgettable safari experiences that connect our guests with the mesmerizing beauty of Zanzibar while fostering a deep appreciation for wildlife conservation and cultural heritage. We strive to create meaningful, impactful journeys that not only delight our guests but also contribute positively to the local communities and ecosystems we explore.

What We Offer

Tailored Safari Experiences We specialize in crafting personalized safari itineraries that cater to the diverse interests and preferences of our clients. Whether you seek the thrill of tracking elusive big game, the serenity of bird-watching, or the cultural richness of local village tours, we design experiences that exceed your expectations.

Expert Guides

Our team of professional guides are not only knowledgeable about the flora and fauna of Zanzibar but are also passionate storytellers who bring the landscape to life. Their expertise ensures that every safari is educational, safe, and profoundly engaging.

Sustainable Tourism

We are deeply committed to sustainable tourism practices. Our safaris are designed to minimize environmental impact, support local conservation efforts, and benefit the communities we visit. We work closely with local partners to ensure that our tours promote the well-being of both the wildlife and the people of Zanzibar.

Luxurious Accommodations

Experience the wild in comfort and style. Our safari packages include stays at some of Zanzibar's finest lodges and camps, offering top-notch amenities, stunning views, and exceptional service. Whether you prefer a rustic tented camp or a luxurious lodge, we provide accommodations that enhance your safari experience.

Our Team

Our dedicated team comprises passionate individuals with a shared love for Zanzibar and its wildlife. From our knowledgeable guides and skilled drivers to our friendly customer service representatives and experienced tour planners, every team member plays a crucial role in delivering an exceptional safari experience. We pride ourselves on our professionalism, attention to detail, and commitment to making your adventure truly unforgettable.

Community and Conservation

Giving back is integral to our ethos. Zanzibar Safari Tours actively supports local conservation initiatives and community projects. We collaborate with local organizations to protect endangered species, promote sustainable practices, and improve the livelihoods of the communities we interact with. By choosing Zanzibar Safari Tours, you are also contributing to the preservation of Zanzibar’s natural and cultural heritage.
